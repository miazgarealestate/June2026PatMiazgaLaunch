Town — Osoyoos

Osoyoos

Canada's warmest town. Osoyoos Lake, wine country, golf, desert. The anchor community of the South Okanagan — and exempt from BC's Speculation & Vacancy Tax.

Oliver — Canada's Wine Capital

The Golden Mile Bench and Black Sage Bench. Burrowing Owl, Black Hills, and 50+ wineries on your doorstep. Also exempt from the Speculation Tax. More affordable than Osoyoos.

The Cottages on Osoyoos Lake

285 homes. 1,800ft of waterfront. Private beach, marina, 2 pools, community centre. Sold-out development — resale only. One of the South Okanagan's most sought-after communities.

Gated 55+ — Osoyoos

Casitas Del Sol

Gated 55+ rancher community on the hillside above Osoyoos. Lake views, backs onto Osoyoos Golf, $120/mo fees. The sensible retirement buy that doesn't compromise on quality of life.

Desert Mirage

Village — Okanagan Falls

Okanagan Falls

"OK Falls" — a smaller, quieter community between Osoyoos and Penticton. Skaha Lake access, growing wine scene, affordable entry, and a genuinely local feel that larger towns have lost.

Village — Similkameen Valley

Keremeos

The fruit stand capital of BC. Orchard country, lower prices, the Similkameen River, and access to some spectacular wilderness. The under-the-radar choice for buyers who want more land for their dollar.
